Ringette Game

Experience the excitement of ringette in this fast-paced game! Control your player, pick up the ring, and score goals while avoiding the goalie.

Download Game

How to Play

  • Use ARROW KEYS or WASD to move your player
  • LEFT CLICK near the ring to pick it up
  • LEFT CLICK while holding to drop the ring
  • SPACE to shoot the ring
  • Mouse position determines shooting direction
  • Score by shooting into the goals
  • Watch out for the 30-second shot clock!
